Computational Studies of Molecular Bismuth Complexes

In progress

Abstract: Computational analysis of effects of ligand architecture on bond strength and radical stability in molecular bismuth complexes through using Density Functional Theory (DFT) and Quasi-Atomic Orbital (QUAO)

Methods: The bond dissociation energy (BDE) calculations through ground-state geometry optimization and vibrational frequency analysis via DFT (M06-L/def2-TZVP/ECP), followed by QUAO analysis of electronic structure (œÉ bonds, delocalized œÄ orbitals, hyperconjugation, œÄ backbonding)
