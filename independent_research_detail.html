Background

PLA-PHB blends offer biodegradability with tunable mechanics. However, increased crystallinity often trades off with toughness and processing ease. We synthesize literature insights to provide practical design heuristics for target applications.

Approach

  • Literature mining and normalization of reported properties across compositions.
  • Simple mapping of crystallinity vs. tensile metrics with processing notes.
  • Heuristic recommendations for application-oriented targets.

Key Takeaways

  • Moderate PHB fraction improves crystallinity while maintaining workable toughness ranges.
  • Nucleation/annealing protocols materially shift property windows; processing discipline matters.
  • Provide shortlists of blend windows for packaging vs. durable biodegradable goods.
